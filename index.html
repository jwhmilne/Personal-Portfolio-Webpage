    About

    • A little bit more about me

      I'm from Sheffield in the north of England in the UK.

      Languages, teaching, and cultural exchange are three of the most valuable things in my life.

      I have a TESOL certificate, a TEFL certificate, and a Bachelor's Degree in Education (with Honours) with a minor in TESOL. Since 2015, I have taught over 80,000 unique students online. Lecturing and reading to students isn't my style, I like to get students involved and I correct their mistakes quickly and effectively.

    Your Online Classes

    • 1-on-1 Lessons


      Each lesson will be tailored to your level and needs as a student. You will improve your speaking fluency, pronunciation, vocabulary, and grammar. Lessons are taught on Zoom or VooV.

    • Homework & Projects


      After each lesson, you will be assigned homework to help maintain consistency with progress. I will mark your homework outside of class times. This is all included in the 1-on-1 lesson fee.

    • Lesson Resources


      I will handpick lesson material according to your level (A1-C2). After each class, you will be able to access the full, recorded class video, audio, and the annotated notes from the material.
